Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This policy is published in English and Turkish. In case of any discrepancy, the English version prevails.

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how Olbiatech Limited (“Olbiatech”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects personal data. Olbiatech is a company registered in England & Wales, located at 17 Green Lanes, London N16 9BS, United Kingdom. For data-protection matters contact contact@olbiatech.com.

We provide AI-powered customer-communication services — including an AI receptionist (voice), automated replies to Instagram, Facebook Messenger and website messages, appointment booking, and lead capture. For these services we may act as a data controller (our own website visitors and clients) and as a data processor (the end-customer data we handle on behalf of our business clients).

2. Information we collect

  • Contact & account details: name, email, phone, company, role.
  • Communications: messages you send us or our clients via website forms, our chat widget, email, phone/voice calls, and connected messaging channels (Instagram and Facebook Messenger).
  • AI inputs & outputs: the content of messages/prompts processed by our systems and the responses generated.
  • End-customer data (on behalf of clients): when a business client uses our services, we process the personal data of their customers (e.g. names, contact details, enquiries, appointment details) strictly to deliver the service.
  • Technical & usage data: IP address, device/browser type, pages viewed, and operational logs.

3. How we use information

  • To provide and operate the services (answer calls and messages, respond, qualify enquiries, and book appointments).
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot.
  • To improve service quality and reliability.
  • To send service or, where permitted, marketing communications (you can opt out at any time).
  • To meet legal and compliance obligations.

4. Platform integrations & third-party data

Meta — Instagram & Messenger

When a business client connects their Facebook Page and/or Instagram professional account, we receive and process messaging content and related metadata in order to provide automated responses and appointment booking on their behalf. We request only the permissions needed for this purpose, and our access to and use of information obtained through Meta’s platforms complies with the Meta Platform Terms and applicable Developer Policies. End-users can request deletion of their data at any time (see “Data deletion” below).

Google — Calendar

When a client connects Google Calendar, we access calendar availability and events solely to check availability and create or update appointments. Olbiatech’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use Google user data for advertising, and we do not sell it.

5. AI processing

Messages handled by our services may be processed by artificial-intelligence models to understand intent and generate responses. AI outputs are designed to assist, not replace, human judgement, and our clients retain oversight of their own communications.

6. Service providers (sub-processors)

We share personal data with a limited set of trusted providers strictly to operate the services:

  • Anthropic: AI language processing (Claude).
  • OpenAI: AI language processing.
  • Retell: AI voice/telephony agent.
  • Twilio: telephony and SMS.
  • GoHighLevel: CRM and marketing automation.
  • Supabase: database & storage.
  • Vercel: website & application hosting.
  • Hostinger: server infrastructure for our automation layer.
  • Meta and Google, where the corresponding integration is enabled by a client.

These providers may engage their own sub-processors under appropriate contractual safeguards. We do not sell personal data.

7. International transfers

We are based in the UK and process data under the UK GDPR. Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses.

8. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. After an account is terminated, we generally retain associated data for up to 30 days and then delete or anonymise it, unless a longer period is legally required.

9. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your personal data, and you may object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, email contact@olbiatech.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

10. Data deletion

To request deletion of your personal data — including data associated with Instagram or Facebook Messenger interactions — please see our Data Deletion Instructions or email contact@olbiatech.com. We action verified requests within 30 days.

11. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, restrict access to those who need it, and store credentials securely.

12. Children

Our services are intended for businesses and are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
